Heritage Timeline

Journey through centuries of Ugandan craftsmanship evolution

Pre-Colonial Era

Before 1800s

Traditional pottery, basketry, and iron working flourished across different kingdoms. Each region developed unique techniques passed down through generations.

Pottery Iron Working Basketry

Colonial Period

1800s - 1962

Introduction of new materials and techniques while traditional crafts adapted to changing markets. Missionary schools began documenting traditional patterns.

Textile Weaving Wood Carving Pattern Documentation

Modern Revival

1990s - Present

Digital platforms and cultural preservation initiatives have revitalized traditional crafts, connecting artisans with global markets while maintaining authenticity.
